Business models of the internet.

In the age of the internet, we have various business models that many large companies use to sell their companies. Ad based Business Model Profit = Ad revenue - Server Cost - Customer Acquisition Cost Freemium Business Model Profit = Premium Subscription - Server Cost - Customer Acquisition Cost Merchandise Business Model Profit = Merchandise - Server Cost - Inventory Costs - Customer Acquisition Cost Enterprise SaaS Business Model Profit = Premium Subscription + Consulting Fees + Training - Server Cost- Customer Acquisition Cost.
