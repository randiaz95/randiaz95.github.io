The Year 2100

In the year 2100 you will think and these thoughts would be observed from a wifi router that is listening to your brain waves by Microwave and Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) in your home.
These thoughts will instantly become software that generate into thousands of possible solutions with enhanced machine learning predictive suggestions.
This router that is interfaced to your brain will then inform a 3 dimensional printer to create the building blocks of your solution. However this is no ordinary printer, the printer does not create solid matter.
This printer will print nanobots with the ability to embed software blueprints into any possible configuration. These nano bots move to the area of application and begin to work instantaneously.
You think: Hey, wouldn't breakfast sound nice?
And by the time you wake up to brush your teeth, you hear bacon sizzling in the background.
While you are eating your warm breakfast, you think of a new design for an electric car and after a short restroom break your car is complete.
This car has all the features you thought of or you are thinking of. Since it has nearly instant compute power.
Work will be a foreign concept; all we have to do is think and no other labor will be necessary.
Our brains will swell with the constant thinking and radiation. This will occur to such a degree that we can instantly communicate with other humans telepathically. Our power to move matter and communicate to other beings telepathically will seem to look alien to us Pre-Telepathic humans.
The world will be different. This is what Elon Musk's NeuraLink Company wants to do.
